---
title: "Submissions"
opening: |
  We welcome contributions from historians, researchers, and writers who share our mission of documenting women's historical contributions.
accept: |
  - Well-researched biographical profiles.
  - Technical deep-dives into specific inventions or theories.
  - Essays on the history of women in specific industries.
decline: |
  - Fiction or creative non-fiction without historical basis.
  - Unverified claims or promotional content.
  - Submissions without proper sourcing.
guidelines: |
  Submissions should be between 800 and 2,000 words, written for a general but curious audience, and include a reference list in APA format.
process: |
  Our editorial team reviews all submissions for historical accuracy and alignment with our editorial standards. You may be asked for revisions before publication.
rights: |
  Authors retain the intellectual rights to their work, but grant The She Archive a non-exclusive license to publish and archive the content digitally.
